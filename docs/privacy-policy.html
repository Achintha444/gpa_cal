Data Collection

We do not collect any data. GPA Cal operates entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, use analytics services, or contain third-party SDKs. There are no cookies, no tracking pixels, and no telemetry of any kind.

Data Storage

All information you enter — your name, university, semesters, courses, grades, and credits — is stored locally on your device only using an on-device database. This data never leaves your phone.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. We don't have access to your data in the first place — it exists only on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app via Settings → Clear All Data. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data from your device.

Permissions

GPA Cal does not request any device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other device feature.

Children's Privacy

GPA Cal does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for students of all ages.

Changes

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
