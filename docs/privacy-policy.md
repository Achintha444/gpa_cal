# Privacy Policy

**GPA Cal** — Last updated: June 14, 2026

## Overview

GPA Cal is a mobile GPA calculator designed for students. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** GPA Cal:

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track usage or analytics
- Does not use cookies
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not contain ads or third-party SDKs

## Data Storage

All data you enter (name, university, semesters, courses, grades, and credits) is stored **locally on your device only** using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have access to your data. Your information stays entirely on your device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app:

**Settings > Clear All Data**

Uninstalling the app will also remove all stored data from your device.

## Permissions

GPA Cal does not request any device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, files, or any other device features.

## Children's Privacy

GPA Cal does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by students of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
